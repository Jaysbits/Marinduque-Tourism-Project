Gasang-Gasang Spotlight

Unlike the solemn Moriones, this is pure energy. Features street-dancing with fast, rhythmic, and joyful movements representing the Resurrection.

Bila-Bila Spotlight

An environmental campaign where students dress as butterflies. It promotes the protection of the Marinduque Wildlife Sanctuary.

2026 Special: The Centenary of Festivals

The provincial government has launched the "Unified Festival Circuit."

📢 Tips for Festival-Goers

  • Book Early: Hotels fill up 3 months in advance for peak festivals.
  • Hydrate: It reaches 34°C during summer; bring a reusable bottle.
  • Wear Local: Buy Buntal hats locally to support our artisans!
