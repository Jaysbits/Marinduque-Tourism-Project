I. Poctoy White Beach – The Mainland Jewel

Often called the "Boracay of Marinduque," Poctoy White Beach in Torrijos is a kilometer-long stretch of white coral sand and clear turquoise waters.

II. The Majestic Backdrop

One of Poctoy’s most iconic features is the unobstructed view of Mount Malindig, creating a dramatic contrast between the blue sea and the lush volcanic slopes.

📸 Pro-Tip: Golden Hour

Visit around 5:00 PM for the most spectacular lighting and photography conditions.

🌿 The "Green Beach" Initiative

Help preserve our jewel. Poctoy is a Plastic-Free Zone. Visitors are encouraged to use reusable containers and respect restricted docking areas to protect our marine sanctuary.
