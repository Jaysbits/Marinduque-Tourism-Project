The 6 Municipalities

Marinduque is divided into six unique districts. From heritage centers and rugged ports to white-sand islands and volcanic peaks, every town offers a different slice of the island's heart.

Historical & Grand

1. Boac

The Heritage Capital

The center of commerce and the main stage for the Moriones Festival. Home to the fortress-like Boac Cathedral.

Must See: Ancestral Houses

Artistic & Scenic

2. Gasan

The Cultural Gateway

An artistic town known for its Nature Park and the Tres Reyes Islands, perfect for divers and snorkelers.

Must See: Tres Reyes Islands

Rugged & Symbolic

3. Mogpog

The Geodetic Center

The birthplace of the original Moriones tradition and the location of the Luzon Datum (Center of the Philippines).

Must See: Balanacan Port

Wild & Mysterious

4. Santa Cruz

The Adventure Hub

The largest municipality. A top destination for white-sand bars (Maniwaya) and deep cave systems.

Must See: Maniwaya Island

Serene & Heroic

5. Torrijos

The Nature Retreat

Famous for its pristine Poctoy White Beach and the historic Pulang Lupa Shrine, a site of Filipino victory.

Must See: Poctoy White Beach

Relaxing & Majestic

6. Buenavista

The Healing Peak

Offers the island’s highest views from Mt. Malindig and therapeutic recovery at the Malbog Sulfur Hot Springs.

Must See: Malbog Hot Springs
