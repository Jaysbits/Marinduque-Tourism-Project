I. The Geodetic Center of the Philippines

Marinduque holds a unique title as the home of the Luzon Datum of 1911, also known as Station Balanacan.

  • What is it? The primary survey point (the "0,0" coordinate) for the entire country.
  • Location: Mount Piit, Mogpog.
  • Importance: Every Philippine map is aligned to this point.
  • Coordinates: 13° 33' N, 121° 52' E

II. Physical Terrain

Marinduque is a volcanic island with a rugged mountainous interior and coastal plains.

  • ❤️ Shape: Heart-shaped (cordiform)
  • 📏 Land Area: Approx. 952.58 sq km
  • ⛰️ Highest Point: Mt. Malindig (1,157m)
  • 🛣️ Coastline: Connected by a scenic circumferential road
Quick Fact

Marinduque is often called the Heart of the Philippines due to its shape and geographical location.
