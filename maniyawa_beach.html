I. Maniwaya Island – The Tropical Escape

Located off the coast of Santa Cruz, Maniwaya is Marinduque's answer to paradise. Powdery white sand, turquoise waters, and a serene atmosphere make it a peaceful alternative to the country's busier beach destinations.

II. The Three Wonders

🏝️ Palad Sandbar

The Vanishing Paradise

A magical stretch of white sand that only appears during low tide. Walk in the middle of the sea with water so clear it looks like glass.

🪨 Ungab Rock Formation

Nature's Architecture

Located on nearby Mongpong Island, this massive natural stone arch is a stunning backdrop for swimming and photography.

🌅 West Coast Sunsets

Daily Light Show

Maniwaya offers unobstructed views of the horizon, where the sky turns into a canvas of deep violet, orange, and gold every evening.

🛶 Island Activities (2026)

  • 🤿 Snorkeling: Explore vibrant coral gardens just a short boat ride from the shore.
  • ⛺ Glamping: Experience luxury camping in "A-frame" cabins or tents under coconut trees.
  • 🏄 Paddleboarding: The calm morning waters are perfect for beginners to test their balance.

📊 Quick Facts

Size:Walkable in ~2 hours

IV. Travel Essentials: How to Get There

Step Route Est. Time
1 Land Travel: Santa Cruz Town to Buyabod Port 20 mins
2 Boat Ride: Motorized banca from Buyabod to Maniwaya 45–60 mins
3 Arrival: Resort pick-up or main beach docking —

💡 Traveler’s Pro-Tips

🌊 Check the Tide: Coordinate with boatmen; the sandbar disappears during high tide.
💸 Cash is King: There are no ATMs on the island. Bring enough for meals and tours.
♻️ Eco-Travel: Maniwaya is a plastic-free advocate. Please bring your trash back to the mainland.
