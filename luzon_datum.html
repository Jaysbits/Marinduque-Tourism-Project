Luzon Datum of 1911

The "Center of the Center"

Located at the summit of Mt. Mataas in Mogpog, this is the primary survey reference point of the Philippines. It serves as the "0,0" coordinate for mapping the entire archipelago.

🚶 The Climb to the Top

  • 🏁 Steps: Approx. 460 concrete steps lead you to the marker.
  • 🌅 The View: Experience a stunning 360° view of Balanacan Port and the Sibuyan Sea.
  • 🙏 Icon: The statue of Our Lady of Biglang Awa stands watch beside the datum marker.

🔬 Why It Matters

  • 🗺️ Accuracy: Before GPS, this point ensured maps across different regions aligned perfectly.
  • 🛰️ Legacy: Even with modern satellite tech, it remains the historical foundation of Philippine geodesy.
  • 📍 Coords: 13° 33' 45.4" N, 121° 52' 04.2" E
💡

Visiting Tips for 2026

Best Time: Start your climb at 6:30 AM to avoid the heat and catch the best morning light.

Pro-Tip: After descending, head to the nearby Paadjao Falls for a refreshing swim!
