I. Tres Reyes Islands – The Royal Trio

Located off the coast of Gasan, this group of three islands is named after the biblical Magi: Gaspar, Melchor, and Baltazar. They represent the most iconic maritime landmarks in western Marinduque.

II. Meet the Kings

The Largest

Gaspar Island

Home to white coral sand beaches and a small local community. It is a famous archaeological site where Ming Dynasty porcelain was recovered.

Best for Picnics
The Rugged

Melchor Island

A dramatic landscape of rugged cliffs and sea caves. Mostly uninhabited, making it the perfect "vibe" for scenic photography and adventure.

Best for Caves
The Deep

Baltazar Island

Famous for its vertical underwater wall and diving spots. It is home to the historic Baltazar Lighthouse, guiding ships for decades.

Best for Diving

III. Diving & Snorkeling (2026)

🛡️ Marine Protected Area
  • The "Wall" Dive: A vertical reef drop featuring vibrant corals, schools of tuna, and occasional barracuda.
  • Pawikan Gardens: Snorkelers at Gaspar Island frequently spot sea turtles (pawikan) grazing on the sea grass.
🤿

Visibility: Up to 20+ Meters
