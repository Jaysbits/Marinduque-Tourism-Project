The 6 Municipalities – A Tour of the Island

Marinduque is divided into six municipalities, each offering a unique slice of the island’s heart, from historical centers to beach paradises and mountain retreats.

1. Boac (The Heritage Capital)

The Vibe: Historical, bustling, and grand.

Must-See: Boac Cathedral (Fortress Church) and Spanish-era ancestral houses.

Claim to Fame: The center of commerce and the main stage for the Moriones Festival.

2. Gasan (The Cultural Gateway)

The Vibe: Artistic and scenic.

Must-See: Tres Reyes Islands—ideal for diving and snorkeling.

Claim to Fame: Home of the Gasang-Gasang Festival and Gasan Nature Park.

3. Mogpog (The Geodetic Center)

The Vibe: Rugged and symbolic.

Must-See: Luzon Datum of 1911 and Balanacan Port.

Claim to Fame: Birthplace of the original Moriones tradition.

4. Santa Cruz (The Adventure Hub)

The Vibe: Wild and mysterious.

Must-See: Maniwaya Island and Bathala Caves.

Claim to Fame: Largest municipality and top destination for beaches and caves.

5. Torrijos (The Nature Retreat)

The Vibe: Serene and heroic.

Must-See: Poctoy White Beach and Pulang Lupa Shrine.

Claim to Fame: Known for its beaches and historical significance.

6. Buenavista (The Healing Peak)

The Vibe: Relaxing and majestic.

Must-See: Mt. Malindig and Malbog Sulfur Hot Springs.

Claim to Fame: Offers scenic views and wellness destinations.
