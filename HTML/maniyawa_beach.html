I. Maniwaya Island – The Tropical Escape

Located off the coast of Santa Cruz, Maniwaya Island is one of the most popular beach destinations in Marinduque. Known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters, it offers a peaceful alternative to crowded tourist spots like Boracay.

II. The Three Wonders of Maniwaya

Palad Sandbar

  • The Magic: Appears only during low tide
  • The Experience: Walk on white sand surrounded by glass-like water

Ungab Rock Formation

  • The Landmark: A natural stone arch on Mongpong Island
  • The Photo-Op: Swim under the arch during high tide

West Coast Sunsets

  • Enjoy breathtaking sunsets with violet and orange skies

III. Island Activities (2026 Guide)

  • Snorkeling: Explore coral gardens near the shore
  • Glamping: Stay in luxury tents under coconut trees
  • Kayaking & Paddleboarding: Calm waters perfect for beginners

V. Traveler’s Pro-Tips

  • Check the Tide: Palad Sandbar disappears during high tide
  • Cash is King: No ATMs available on the island
  • Leave No Trace: Bring your trash back to the mainland

VI. Quick Facts

  • Island Size: Can be explored in an afternoon
  • Best Time: February to May (summer season)
  • Fresh Catch: Try grilled squid and sea urchin from local fishermen
