II. Spotlight: Gasang-Gasang Festival (Easter Sunday)

Unlike the solemn Moriones, Gasang-Gasang is pure energy.

  • The Dance: Street-dancing competition between barangay contingents
  • The Theme: Celebrates the Resurrection of Christ with fast, rhythmic, joyful movements

III. Spotlight: Bila-Bila (Butterfly) Festival

  • The Parade: Students dress as larvae, pupae, and butterflies
  • The Message: Environmental campaign to protect the Marinduque Wildlife Sanctuary

IV. 2026 Special Event: The Centenary of Festivals

The provincial government launched a "Unified Festival Circuit" in 2026.

  • Traveler's Tip: Marinduque Expo at Boac Riverside showcases local crafts like Buntal weaving and Nitto basketry

V. Monthly "Mini-Festivals"

  • December 8: Boac – Feast of the Immaculate Conception
  • May 1: Santa Cruz – Feast of the Holy Cross
  • May 15: Gasan – Feast of St. Joseph

VI. Tips for Festival-Goers

  • Book Early: Hotels fill up 3 months in advance for Moriones and Gasang-Gasang
  • Hydrate: Bring a reusable water bottle during 34°C summer heat
  • Wear Local: Support the economy by buying Buntal hats or festival shirts
