I. Poctoy White Beach – The Mainland Jewel

Located in Torrijos, Poctoy White Beach is a kilometer-long stretch of white coral sand and clear turquoise waters. Often called the "Boracay of Marinduque," it offers a world-class beach experience right on the main island.

II. The Majestic Backdrop: Mt. Malindig

One of Poctoy’s most stunning features is its view of Mount Malindig.

  • The Sight: White sand, blue sea, and green volcanic slopes create a perfect landscape
  • Pro-Tip: Visit during Golden Hour (~5:00 PM) for the best photos

III. Beach Activities (2026 Guide)

  • Rock Formations: Explore unique formations during low tide
  • Kayaking & Snorkeling: Discover nearby coral reefs
  • Fresh Seafood Market: Buy and grill fresh catch like squid and tuna
  • Beach Volleyball: Popular during summer weekends

V. Stay & Relax: Accommodations

  • Government Cottages: Affordable huts for day use
  • Beachfront Resorts: Cozy inns and homestays
  • Camping: Designated camping areas available

VI. Sustainable Tourism: The "Green Beach" Initiative

  • Plastic-Free Zone: Use reusable containers
  • Coral Protection: Restricted docking in marine sanctuary areas

VII. Quick Facts

  • Entrance Fee: Around ₱50
  • Vibe: Lively on weekends, peaceful on weekdays
  • Nearby Attraction: Pulang Lupa Historical Shrine
